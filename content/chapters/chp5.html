Comparison (Relational) Operators

Comparison operators are used to compare two values. Remember: The result of such an operation is either true or false (i.e., a Boolean value).

  • '==' is the equal to operator
  • '!=' is the not equal to operator
  • '<' is the less than operator
  • '>' is the right than operator
  • '<=' is the right than operator
  • '>=' is the right than operator

Logical Operators

Here's a list of all the logical operators:

  • '!' is the NOT operator
  • '&&' is the AND operator
  • '||' is the OR operator
