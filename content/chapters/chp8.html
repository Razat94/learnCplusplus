Loops

Up to this point, your programs may notice yourself repeating the same actions over and over again. In cases like these, let the computer do the hard work for you! Loops are ways to repeat a block of code a certain number of times, or while a condition is fulfilled.

There are 3 main types of loops:

  • For Loops
  • While Loops
  • Do-While Loops
